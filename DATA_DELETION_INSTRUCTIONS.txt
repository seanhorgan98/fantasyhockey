Data Deletion Instructions Draft

GUHMC Fantasy uses Facebook services for login purposes and only has access to your public profile
and email adress.
According to Facebook policy, we have to provide either a USER DATA DELETION CALLBACK or
a USER DATA DELETION INSTRUCTION URL. 
You can remove GUMHC Fantasy's persmission to capture data by following these steps:

1. Go to your Facebook account settings. Account -> Settings

2. Find the "Apps and Websites" section

3. Search for and then select "GUMHC Fantasy"

4. Select the "Remove" option 

5. Choose your preferences on the pop up and click "OK" to complete

NOTE: Removing the app from your account will stop any further data being captured by the
app but will not automatically delete any data already stored. To request that your stored
data be deleted, please contact us via email.
